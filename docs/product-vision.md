# Product Vision

## Purpose

Datapex Connect is a professional identity platform for enterprise meetings, conference presentations, and customer follow-up.

It is not merely a digital business card. Its purpose is to present a person, the organisations they represent, and the solutions they can discuss in a way that feels credible, practical, and ready for business conversation.

## What the product should do

Datapex Connect should help a visitor answer these questions quickly:

1. Who is this person?
2. What do they do?
3. Which companies and solutions are associated with them?
4. How do I continue the conversation?

The current product direction is centred on a profile experience that combines identity, affiliations, featured solutions, and direct communication actions.

## Product model

The current system represents:

- profiles as structured people content
- companies as structured affiliation content
- solutions as curated presentation content associated with the profile experience

This allows Datapex Connect to grow from one profile into a repeatable identity platform without changing the overall experience model.

## Why content-driven architecture matters

The product needs to support future additions such as:

- multiple profiles
- QR code entry points
- downloadable vCards
- wallet passes
- event-specific landing experiences

Those needs are easier to support when identity data lives in structured content rather than inside page components.

## MVP scope

The current MVP should remain focused on:

- one high-quality professional profile experience
- clear contact actions
- company affiliations
- featured solutions
- documentation and structure that support future expansion

## Out of scope for the current MVP

The following should stay out of scope until the core identity flow is proven:

- CMS or admin editing tools
- CRM integration
- analytics dashboards
- scheduling flows
- authentication
- multi-event management UI
- wallet pass implementation
- QR code generation implementation

## Near-term expansion path

Once the MVP is stable, the same platform can be extended to support:

- more people profiles
- profile-specific vCard generation
- QR-driven conference access
- wallet pass delivery
- event-specific messaging, solutions, and branding
